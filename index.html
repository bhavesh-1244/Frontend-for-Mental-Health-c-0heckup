Mental Health Quiz

Q1: How often do you feel nervous or anxious?






Q2: How often do you experience sudden fear or panic without an apparent reason?






Q3: How often do you feel sad or hopeless?






Q4: How often do you feel tired or without energy, even after resting?






Q5: How often do you have mood swings?






Q6: Do you ever feel extremely energetic or euphoric for extended periods, followed by feeling very down?






Q7: Do you avoid situations that remind you of a traumatic event?






Q8: Do you experience flashbacks or nightmares about a traumatic event?






Q9: How often do you have repetitive thoughts or behaviors?






Q10: Do you feel compelled to perform certain rituals or routines, even if they seem unnecessary?
